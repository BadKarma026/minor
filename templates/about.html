As a home cook, I often wonder what to make for a weeknight dinner, on my weekly grocery runs. More often than not, I find myself scrolling through my phone at the supermarket, searching for a new recipe to try, but end up making the same repetitive handful of meals.

Why not spice my life up with personalized recipe recommendations?

With thousands of recipes, this application helps with discovering relevant content, learning new cuisines/culinary techniques, and preparing for future grocery trips!
